About

A few true things about my work.

I originated the prototype that became Trailhead, the platform that's onboarded millions of admins and developers into the Salesforce ecosystem. I created and hosted the Salesforce Developer Podcast on a single editorial rule: give the mic to people who were making interesting things and didn't have one.

Five years as a crisis line operator informs how I think about product, language, and the difference between sounding helpful and being helpful. Looking for individual-contributor work at a mission-driven organization, close to the people I'm building for.
